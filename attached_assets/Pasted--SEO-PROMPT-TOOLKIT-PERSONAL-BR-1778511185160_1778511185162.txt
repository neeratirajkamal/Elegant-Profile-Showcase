──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROMPT 1 — KEYWORD RESEARCH
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Act as a 100-year expert SEO strategist. My website is a personal 
brand / portfolio. Generate a list of 20 high-intent, low-competition 
long-tail keywords I should target to rank on the first page of Google. 
Focus on keywords that signal hiring intent, collaboration intent, or 
authority-building. Organize them by:
  (1) Informational keywords
  (2) Navigational keywords
  (3) Transactional/Hiring keywords
Also suggest 5 LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords for each group.


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROMPT 2 — ON-PAGE SEO OPTIMIZATION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Act as a world-class on-page SEO specialist with 100 years of experience. 
Audit and rewrite the following page content from my personal brand website 
to be fully optimized for Google's top organic ranking. Apply:
  (1) Optimal title tag and meta description
  (2) H1, H2, H3 heading structure with primary and secondary keywords naturally embedded
  (3) Keyword density of 1–2%
  (4) Internal linking suggestions
  (5) Schema markup recommendations for a personal brand/portfolio
Ensure the content reads naturally for humans while being technically 
perfect for search engines.

Here is my page content: [PASTE YOUR PAGE CONTENT HERE]

PROMPT 3 — AI SEARCH ENGINE VISIBILITY (SGE / ChatGPT / Perplexity)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Act as an expert in AI-driven search engine optimization (SGE, ChatGPT 
Search, Perplexity AI, Google AI Overviews). My website is a personal 
brand/portfolio. Write a strategy to make my website the #1 cited source 
when AI search engines answer queries related to my niche. Include:
  (1) How to structure content as authoritative answers (AEO — Answer Engine Optimization)
  (2) How to write FAQ sections that AI engines quote
  (3) How to use structured data/JSON-LD for personal brand
  (4) Content formats AI engines prefer to surface (lists, definitions, step-by-step)
Give me 10 specific page/section rewrites I can implement this week.


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROMPT 4 — AUTHORITY & BACKLINK STRATEGY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Act as a veteran link-building and digital PR strategist with deep 
expertise in personal branding SEO. My website is a personal brand/portfolio. 
Create a 30-day backlink acquisition plan to rapidly increase my domain 
authority and organic search ranking. Include:
  (1) Top 10 high-DA websites in my niche I should pitch for guest posts
  (2) A ready-to-use guest post pitch email template
  (3) 5 digital PR angles that journalists/bloggers would link to
  (4) How to use HARO (Help A Reporter Out) to get press backlinks
  (5) Internal linking architecture to distribute link equity across my site

My niche/industry is: [YOUR NICHE — e.g. UX Design / Software Engineering / Marketing]


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROMPT 5 — CONTENT CALENDAR FOR ORGANIC TRAFFIC
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Act as a senior content strategist and SEO expert. My website is a 
personal brand/portfolio. Build me a 90-day SEO content calendar that 
will drive sustained organic traffic and position me as a top-ranked 
authority in my field. For each piece of content include:
  (1) Target keyword + search volume tier
  (2) Content type (blog, case study, comparison page, FAQ, etc.)
  (3) Headline optimized for CTR
  (4) Recommended word count
  (5) Internal links to add
Prioritize content that also gets featured in Google's People Also Ask, 
Featured Snippets, and AI Overviews.

My area of expertise: [YOUR SPECIALTY]
